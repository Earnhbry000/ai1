How I Make EAS Alarms

So, I see you came here to find out how I make my EAS Alarms. These are something I do for fun so I will tell you how. Hope you like this Documentation.

Things You Will Need

You Will Need:
  • IMovie (In My Case) Or Any Video Editing Software
  • EAS Sounds(Avalible At Bottom Of Page)
  • Your EAS Message (Instructions On Next Step)
    • You Will Also Need These Websites:
  • Ttsdemo.com
  • PhotoMosh.com (Optional)

    • Step 1: Make Your Voice

    So, On this step you need to go to Ttsdemo.com. On that site do not look at the dog or person that is talking. Instead, Look to the Left Side. There is a voice selection option, select Tom (US).
    There Is a character limit of 600 on this site but you can bypass it by copying and pasting a script from a notepad or using the text box in the cite (will not bypass it). Please Make sure to use "," for a short pause, "." for a longer pause, and ",." for the longest pause in your script.
